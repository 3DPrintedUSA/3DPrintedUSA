Our Materials & Process

At 3DPrintedUSA, we've developed our own proprietary PLA+ composite, blending high-grade PLA with stainless steel particles and carbon fiber strands. Each product is printed at 100% infill, stress-tested for 24 hours, manually inspected, and discreetly packaged. Bitcoin-only payments ensure privacy and eliminate chargebacks.
