Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from 3DPrintedUSA

Is this website legitimate? Will I actually receive my order?

Yes, 3DPrintedUSA is a legitimate marketplace you can trust. We maintain a 99.8% delivery success rate with thousands of satisfied customers. All orders are professionally assembled by our technicians and shipped via LLC partnerships with guaranteed tracking.

What is the $50 Security Package and do I need it?

The Security Package provides comprehensive insurance against theft, loss, or damage during shipping. For $50 per product, you're fully protected. If anything happens to your shipment, we immediately send a replacement. You can remove this option if you prefer, but it's recommended for complete peace of mind.

How do I receive tracking information after ordering?

Tracking information is automatically emailed to the address you provide in the Bitcoin transaction description. Include your email address in the transaction memo when sending payment. You'll receive tracking within 24-48 hours of payment confirmation.

Are the products assembled or do I need to build them?

All products are professionally assembled by our technicians in the USA. We don't sell parts or kits - everything arrives ready for use after professional assembly, testing, and quality control.
